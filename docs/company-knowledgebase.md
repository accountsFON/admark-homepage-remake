## 4. Differentiators

These are the four differentiators ADMARK explicitly promotes:

1. **No contracts** - Month-to-month. No long-term commitments. You stay because results speak for themselves.
2. **Low barrier to entry** - No large upfront financial commitment required. They scale with you.
3. **No commission-based schemes** - Recommendations are driven by what works, not by what pays them more.
4. **Realistic goals** - No outlandish promises. Honest projections and campaigns that deliver.

### Implicit differentiators (from site tone and content)
5. **Full-service under one roof** - Strategy + ads + social + web + creative all in-house
6. **Jacksonville roots** - Local agency, not a remote-only shop. In-person access to the team.
7. **Industry breadth** - 65+ clients across diverse verticals. They've seen your market before.
8. **Responsiveness** - Multiple testimonials call out how responsive and communicative the team is.

## 6. Brand Direction

### Voice & tone
- **Professional but approachable** - Not corporate, not casual. The tone of a knowledgeable friend who happens to be really good at marketing.
- **Confident without arrogance** - "Simply put, we're better" is the boldest the copy gets. Otherwise it's earned confidence backed by proof.
- **Direct** - Short sentences, clear claims. No marketing jargon or fluff.
- **Jacksonville proud** - Local identity is part of the brand. They're a Jacksonville agency, not trying to be a national player.

### Visual direction (from current site)
- **Dark navy/charcoal** as the primary dark tone
- **Teal/cyan** as the accent color (CTAs, highlights)
- **Clean and modern** but not minimal. Content-rich pages with visual hierarchy.
- **Photography-forward** - Team photos, client work, project images throughout
- **Card-based layouts** for services, projects, testimonials

### What to avoid
- Corporate sterility (they're a friendly local agency, not a Fortune 500)
- Overpromising language ("guaranteed results," "10x your revenue")
- Aggressive sales tactics (no urgency timers, no scarcity plays)
- Template/generic stock photo feel
