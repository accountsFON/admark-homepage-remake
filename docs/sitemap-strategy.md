## Page Structure: Homepage

**URL:** `/` (index.html)
**Conversion role:** Awareness + Decision (single-page, must do both)
**SEO intent:** "marketing agency jacksonville" (primary), brand terms
**Primary CTA:** "Get Your Free Consult" / "Get Started"

## Section Map (top to bottom)

### Section 1: Hero
| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Type** | Hero (full-viewport, dark background) |
| **Purpose** | Immediately answer: What is this? Who is it for? Why should I care? Establish Jacksonville identity and trust signal. |
| **Brief** | Lead with the trust-based positioning ("The agency small businesses trust"). Support with the one-stop-shop value prop. Dual CTA: primary action (free consult) and secondary (see work). Include a trust signal element (65+ clients served with avatar stack). Jacksonville geo-tag as an eyebrow. |
| **Image direction** | No hero image. Abstract geometric background (concentric circles, gradient glow). The typography and layout do the heavy lifting. Dark navy gradient background with subtle teal glow accents. |
| **CTA** | Primary: "Free Consult" (arrow icon). Secondary: "See Our Work" (ghost button). |

### Section 2: Strategy Framework (Services Intro)
| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Type** | Services Grid (3-column cards) |
| **Purpose** | Explain the approach, not just the services. Build credibility through methodology. Strategy > Tactics > Results framework shows they think before they do. |
| **Brief** | Eyebrow: "What We Do". Headline about reaching the right people. Three cards: Strategy (market research, audience targeting, brand positioning), Tactics (Google Ads, social ads, SEO), Results (performance tracking, monthly reporting, continuous optimization). Each card gets an icon, title, short paragraph, and a checklist of 3 specifics. |
| **Image direction** | Icon-based. Compass for Strategy, Target for Tactics, Trending Up for Results. Teal icon on light teal background circle. |
| **CTA** | None in this section (the cards themselves build interest). |

### Section 3: Full Capabilities
| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Type** | Feature Grid (dark background, pill/tag layout) |
| **Purpose** | Comprehensive service list. After the strategic framework, show the full breadth. Proves the "one-stop shop" claim. |
| **Brief** | Eyebrow: "Full-Service Capabilities". Headline: "Everything your business needs." Grid of 8 service pills/tags with icons: Google Ads, Social Media Marketing, Web Design, Photography, Videography, Graphic Design, SEO, Marketing Strategy. Dark navy background with subtle radial gradient. Each pill has an icon + label. |
| **Image direction** | Icon-based. One icon per service. Teal icons on dark semi-transparent background pills with subtle border. |
| **CTA** | None (section acts as a trust-building proof point). |

### Section 4: Featured Work
| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Type** | Portfolio Grid (3-column cards) |
| **Purpose** | Show, don't tell. Recognizable client names (Tim Tebow Foundation, Beck Auto Group) build instant credibility. Service tags show breadth. |
| **Brief** | Eyebrow: "Featured Work". Headline: "Projects we're proud of." 6 project cards in a 3x2 grid. Each card: styled placeholder (client name as display text on dark gradient background), client name, and service tags as pills. Cards: Beck Auto Group, Tim Tebow Foundation, William Carey University, SkyLife Elite, Pedal Pub Jacksonville, Campione Law. |
| **Image direction** | No real project images available for this remake. Use typographic card placeholders (client name in serif font on navy gradient). Service tags in teal pills below. |
| **CTA** | None (the work speaks for itself; the next sections drive action). |

### Section 5: Industries
| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Type** | Feature Grid (2x3 icon cards on light background) |
| **Purpose** | Reassure prospects that ADMARK has worked in their vertical before. Reduces risk perception ("they understand my business"). |
| **Brief** | Eyebrow: "Industries". Headline: "We know your market." Supporting text about deep experience across verticals. 6 industry cards: Home Services, Medical & Wellness, Non-Profit & Education, Political, Retail & Restaurant, And More. Each card: icon, title, short descriptor of sub-verticals. |

| **CTA** | None. |

Right column: 4 stacked feature cards, each with icon + title + 1-2 sentence description. Cards: No Contracts, Low Barrier to Entry, No Commission Schemes, Realistic Goals. |

|-------|-------|

## Cross-Section Notes

- **Background alternation:** Hero (dark) > Services (white) > Capabilities (dark) > Work (white) > Industries (cream/light) > Differentiators (white) > Testimonials (dark) > Contact (white) > Footer (dark). No two consecutive sections share the same background.
- **CTA consistency:** Primary CTA text is "Get Your Free Consult" or "Free Consult" everywhere. The nav uses "Get Started" as a shorter variant.
- **Proof distribution:** Testimonials are concentrated in Section 7 (not scattered), but the hero trust signal (65+ clients) provides early social proof.
- **Mobile behavior:** All grids collapse to single-column. Nav becomes hamburger. Hero stacks vertically. Form goes full-width.
