# AD Marketing - Copy Wireframe (Homepage)

> **Messaging blueprint for every section.** Each section gets headline options, body direction, proof points, CTA copy, and tone calibration. This is not final polished copy. It's the strategic framework a copywriter (or the page-build skill) writes from.

---

## Messaging Framework

### Voice Calibration

| Adjective | In Practice | Avoid |
|-----------|------------|-------|
| **Confident** | "Simply put, we're better." Bold claims backed by proof. | Arrogant, braggy, chest-thumping without substance |
| **Approachable** | "Tell us about your project." Conversational, not formal. | Overly casual, slang, trying too hard to be hip |
| **Direct** | Short sentences. Clear claims. No hedge words. | Jargon, buzzwords, "synergy"-type filler |
| **Trustworthy** | Lead with transparency (no contracts, no commissions). | Hype, urgency tactics, "limited time" language |
| **Local** | Jacksonville identity woven in naturally. | Over-indexing on location at the expense of competence |

### Voice Spectrum by Section

| Section | Tone Register |
|---------|--------------|
| Hero | Confident and clear. The billboard. Every word earns its place. |
| Services | Knowledgeable and methodical. Show the thinking behind the doing. |
| Capabilities | Matter-of-fact. Breadth speaks for itself. |
| Featured Work | Understated pride. Let the names do the talking. |
| Industries | Reassuring expertise. "We've done this before." |
| Differentiators | Bold and direct. This is where they make their strongest claims. |
| Testimonials | Client voices (authentic, unedited feel). |
| Contact | Warm and inviting. Reduce friction. No pressure. |

### Messaging Hierarchy

1. **Primary message:** AD Marketing is the marketing agency small businesses trust, because they deliver real results without contracts, commissions, or BS.
2. **Supporting messages (ranked by impact):**
   - One-stop shop: strategy + ads + social + web + creative under one roof
   - No contracts, no commission schemes, low barrier to entry
   - 65+ clients across diverse industries in Jacksonville and beyond
   - Responsive, communicative team that treats you like a partner
3. **Objection map:**
   - "Agencies are too expensive" > Low barrier to entry, no large upfront commitment
   - "I've been burned before" > No contracts, realistic goals, testimonials about trust
   - "They won't understand my business" > Industry breadth (6 verticals), specific client examples
   - "I just need one thing, not a full agency" > They handle individual services too, but the value is integration

### StoryBrand Mapping

| Element | ADMARK Application |
|---------|-------------------|
| **Hero (customer)** | A small business owner in Jacksonville who needs marketing help but doesn't have the time, expertise, or budget for a full marketing department. |
| **Villain (problem)** | External: Marketing is complex, time-consuming, and fragmented across too many tools/vendors. Internal: "I don't know if this will actually work. I've been burned before." Philosophical: Small businesses deserve the same quality marketing as big companies. |
| **Guide (ADMARK)** | Empathy: "We know you've been burned. We know budgets are tight. We get it." Authority: 65+ clients, Tim Tebow Foundation, William Carey University, 9 glowing testimonials. |
| **Plan** | 1. We build your strategy (who to target, what to say). 2. We execute the right tactics (ads, social, web, creative). 3. We track results and optimize (monthly reporting, continuous improvement). |
| **CTA** | Direct: "Get Your Free Consult." Transitional: "See Our Work." |
| **Success** | More leads, more customers, more growth. Marketing that finally works. A partner you can trust. |
| **Failure** | Wasted budget on the wrong channels. More months of inconsistent marketing. Falling behind competitors who are investing. |

### Section 1: HERO

**H-tag:** H1
**SEO keyword:** "marketing agency jacksonville"

**Headline options:**
1. **"The agency small businesses trust."** (Recommended. Matches current site's strongest line. Trust-first positioning. Clean, bold, memorable.)
2. "Your marketing. One team. Real results." (Benefit-focused, emphasizes the one-stop-shop value.)
3. "Jacksonville's marketing partner for small business." (Local + relationship angle. More descriptive, less punchy.)

**Subheadline:**
"Your one-stop shop for marketing that actually works. Strategy, ads, social, web, and creative, all under one roof."

**Body content direction:**
- No body paragraph in hero. The subheadline carries the supporting message.
- Trust signal element below CTAs: "65+ Clients Served / Across Jacksonville and beyond" with avatar stack of client initials.
- Eyebrow above headline: Jacksonville, FL geo-tag with pulse indicator.

**Proof points:** 65+ clients served (logo wall count). Avatar initials from notable clients (BG = Beck Group, TT = Tim Tebow, WC = William Carey, PP = Pedal Pub).

**CTA:**
- Primary: "Free Consult" + right arrow icon. Links to #contact.
- Secondary: "See Our Work" ghost button. Links to #work.
- Supporting text: None in hero (clean, not cluttered).

**Image direction:** No image. Dark navy gradient background with subtle decorative elements (concentric circles, teal glow spots). Let typography and whitespace dominate. Scroll indicator at bottom center.

**Tone:** Confident, clean, bold. Every word earns its place. This is the billboard.

### Section 2: SERVICES (Strategy Framework)

**H-tag:** H2
**SEO keyword:** "marketing strategy small business"

**Headline options:**
1. **"Reach the right people, the right way."** (Recommended. Directly from current site. Clear promise, implies methodology.)
2. "Strategy first. Results always." (Emphasizes the strategic approach over tactical execution.)
3. "Built on strategy. Driven by results." (Similar angle, more polished.)

**Subheadline:**
"A strong marketing strategy is the backbone of any successful business. We walk you through every step to make sure your campaigns target the right customers."

**Body content direction (3 cards):**

**Card 1: Strategy**
- Icon: Compass
- Headline: "Strategy"
- Body: We build your marketing strategy from the ground up. Identify ideal customer. Craft the message that resonates. Every campaign starts here.
- Checklist: Market research, Audience targeting, Brand positioning
- Tone: Methodical, confident

**Card 2: Tactics**
- Icon: Target
- Headline: "Tactics"
- Body: Strategy is only as strong as the tactics that bring it to life. We pick the platforms and frequency that fit your budget and goals.
- Checklist: Google Ads, Social media ads, SEO & content
- Tone: Practical, knowledgeable

**Card 3: Results**
- Icon: Trending Up
- Headline: "Results"
- Body: Committed to long-term success, not short-term vanity metrics. Real growth means customers who keep coming back.
- Checklist: Performance tracking, Monthly reporting, Continuous optimization
- Tone: Steady, honest

**Proof points:** The framework itself is the proof (shows thinking, not just doing).

**CTA:** None. Section builds interest; the work section below provides evidence.

**Image direction:** Icon-based only. Each card gets a Lucide icon in a teal-tinted background circle.

**Tone:** Knowledgeable and methodical. "Here's how we think about this."

### Section 8: CONTACT / CTA

**H-tag:** H2

**Headline options:**
1. **"Tell us about your project."** (Recommended. Inviting, not pushy. From current site.)
2. "Ready to grow? Let's talk." (More direct, assumes intent.)
3. "Your free consult is one form away." (Clear value prop, slightly more transactional.)

**Subheadline:**
"Ready to grow? Get a free consult call, no strings attached. We'll talk through your goals and see if we're the right fit."

**Body content direction (split layout):**

**Left column (CTA copy + contact methods):**
- Paragraph reinforcing the no-pressure, free consult offer
- Three contact methods with icons:
  - Email: hello@admarkjax.com (with envelope icon, hover-to-fill)
  - Phone: 1-904-718-4439 (with phone icon, hover-to-fill)
  - Location: Jacksonville, FL (with pin icon, static)

**Right column (form):**
- Form fields: First Name, Last Name (side by side), Email, Phone, Message (textarea)
- Submit: "Get Your Free Consult" + arrow icon (full-width teal button)
- Success state: Checkmark icon + "We'll be in touch!" + "Thanks for reaching out. Our team will get back to you within 24 hours."

**Proof points:** The low-friction form itself is the proof of "low barrier to entry." No required budget field, no qualification questions. Just tell us about your project.

**CTA:** "Get Your Free Consult" with right arrow. Full-width button. Teal gradient.

**Image direction:** No imagery. Clean form card on cream background. Contact method icons are teal with hover-to-teal-fill interaction.

**Tone:** Warm and inviting. Reduce friction. "No strings attached" and "see if we're the right fit" signal humility and respect for the prospect's time.

## Cross-Page Consistency Notes

- **Primary CTA text is "Get Your Free Consult" everywhere** except the nav which uses the shorter "Get Started"
- **Teal is the only accent color** used for CTAs, icons, links, and highlights
- **No section uses more than one CTA** (keeps the page focused)
- **Testimonial quotes are shortened** to the strongest single sentence, not full reproductions
- **All icons from Lucide library** for consistency
- **Eyebrows are ALL CAPS, teal, small, tracking-wide** on every section
